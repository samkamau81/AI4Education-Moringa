Welcome to EduSign!

Sign Language for Inclusive Learning


EduSign is a groundbreaking initiative dedicated to creating a more inclusive world for the deaf and hard-of-hearing community. We use advanced video processing and machine learning algorithms to translate spoken language and captions into sign language, ensuring that no one is left out of the conversation.

Here is how it works

Video Processing: Our system analyzes video content, identifying spoken language and corresponding captions. Machine Learning: Advanced algorithms translate this information into accurate sign language. Sign Language Output: The translated sign language is then displayed, ensuring that deaf and hard-of-hearing individuals can fully understand the content.
